Breast Cancer 2020

Breast cancer occurs when cells in the breast divide and grow without their normal control. All women – and some men – are at risk for breast cancer. This risk increases with age. Now is the time to know what to look for and what you need to do to take control of your health.

Learn the symptoms.

  • A new lump in the breast or underarm

  • Pain in any area of the breast

  • Swelling of part of the breast

  • Irritation or dimpling of breast skin

  • Redness or flaky skin in the nipple area or the breast

  • Nipple discharge other than breast milk, including blood

  • Any change in the size or the shape of the breast
